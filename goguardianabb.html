GoGuardian AB Blocker [GUIDE]

How to prevent go guardian from seeing about:blank or file urls :D

Sorry this isn't a streamlined process, you can't really do that.

Okay, step 1. go to chrome://extensions and scroll down til you see "GoGuardian".

Note: if it doesn't appear make sure your doing this on your chromebook. not a computer logged into your account.

Step 2 (final step), scroll down and make sure "Allow access to file URLs" is set to false.

Boom, your done. now goguardian can't see any tabs with both file urls or about:blank urls (classroom6x uses them)
